About us

"EIGHT LIMBSS"

urban athletics

Founder- Chai H. Saetern A.K.A. B-boy Swellz

"I started this brands because I got tired of buying overseas product from American company's that were priced way too high and made the rich richer. After thinking about it over and over..There was this idea that my buddies and I started years ago in Sacramento, CA while sitting around in one of their living room. All three of us were complaining about our injuries from breaking (break dancing), so we started brainstorming.. All of this was through words and nothing was written down or anything. Just collaborating ideas and sharing in the moment. It wasn't until July 2017 is when I first started cutting out the blueprint and soon after, I brought it to a local seamstress. I pitched her the idea of what I wanted done and gave her the cutout fabric (Lycra). And then "Eight Limbss" was formed. The very first breaking garment that's handcrafted in Minneapolis, MN with pride, respect, and quality!"

Mission Statment

"Eight Limbss is an urban athletic brand that takes innovation, culture, and fashion to the summit of MOVEMENT. Handcrafted with quality and passion!"
